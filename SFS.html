SOLAR POLAR FIELD STRENGTH

This section is dedicated to solar polar field strength (SPFS).
Daily updated data are taken from Wilcox Solar Observatory.
Data are updated about every 10 days.
to take a screenshot press the button on the lower left side of the canvas.
You can select data range manually from dropdown menu or directly from the dinamic plot.
To download updated data and plot (in .root format) press the button ROOT File.

Solar Polar Field Strength values (North and South) are also avalaible in a 20 Hz low pass filtered format to eliminate yearly geometric projection effects.
Select the values to plot:

North      North 20 Hz      Avg     
South      South 20 Hz      Avg 20 Hz
